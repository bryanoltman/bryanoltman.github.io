How it works: This page measures the time between consecutive pointermove events reported by your browser. With "coalesced events" enabled, it reads every hardware report the browser received between frames via getCoalescedEvents(), giving a truer picture of hardware polling rate.

Tips: Move the mouse in steady circles or figure-eights inside the box. Avoid stopping — pauses inflate the measured interval. Wireless mice, Bluetooth, and power-saving modes may reduce the effective rate. Results depend on browser, OS compositor, and USB/wireless stack.
